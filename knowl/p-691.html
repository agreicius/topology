Observe first that basis elements are open in the topology \(\mathcal{T}\text{,}\) essenitally by definition: i.e., we have \(\mathcal{B}\subseteq \mathcal{T}\text{.}\) The forward implication \((\implies)\) follows easily, since \(\mathcal{T}\subseteq \mathcal{T}'\) and \(\mathcal{B}\subseteq \mathcal{T}\) implies \(\mathcal{B}\subseteq \mathcal{T}'\text{.}\)
