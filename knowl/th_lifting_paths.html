Theorem 1.4.3. Lifting paths.

Let \(p\colon E\rightarrow B\) be a covering map. Fix \(e_0\in E\) and let \(b_0=p(e_0)\text{.}\) Every path \(f\colon I\rightarrow B\) satisfying \(f(0)=b_0\text{,}\) lifts uniquely to a path \(\widetilde{f}\colon I\rightarrow E\) satisfying \(\widetilde{f}(0)=e_0\text{.}\)
