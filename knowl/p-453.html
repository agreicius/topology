\((1)\implies (2)\text{.}\) If \(G\) is free abelian, there is an isomorphism \(\phi\colon \bigoplus_{\alpha\in I}\Z\rightarrow G\text{.}\) Let \(\phi_\alpha=\phi\circ i_\alpha\) and let \(g_{\alpha}=\phi_\alpha(1)\text{.}\) From (1.12.2) we have
\begin{align*} \phi((m_\alpha))\amp = \sum_{\alpha\in I} \phi_\alpha(m_\alpha) \\ \amp = \sum_{\alpha\in I}m_\alpha\phi_\alpha(1) \amp (\phi_\alpha \text{ a homo.})\\ \amp =\sum_{\alpha\in I}m_\alpha g_\alpha\text{.} \end{align*}
