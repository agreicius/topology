Corollary 1.15.4. Seifert-van Kampen:classical.

Let \(X=U\cup V\) be an open covering where \(U\text{,}\) \(V\text{,}\) and \(U\cap V\) are path connected. With notation as in the Seifert-van Kampen theorem, we have
\begin{equation*} \pi_1(X,x_0)\cong \pi_1(U,x_0)*\pi_1(V,x_0)/N\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(N\) is the least normal subgroup of \(\pi_1(U,x_0)*\pi_1(V,x_0)\) containing the set
\begin{equation*} \{(i_1([f]))^{-1}i_2([f])\colon [f]\in \pi_1(U\cap V, x_0)\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
