Fix \(n\geq 1\text{.}\) The \(n\)-sphere \(S^n\) is defined as
\begin{equation*} S^n=\{\boldx\in \R^{n+1}\colon \norm{\boldx}=1\}=\{(x_1,x_2,\dots, x_{n+1})\colon \sqrt{x_1^2+x_2^2+\cdots +x_{n+1}^2}=1\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Prove: \(S^n\) is connected for all \(n\geq 1\text{.}\)
