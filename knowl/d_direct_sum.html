Definition 1.12.5. Direct sum of abelian groups.

Given a family \(\{A_\alpha\}_{\alpha\in I}\) of abelian groups, its direct sum \(\oplus_{\alpha\in I}A_\alpha\) is the subgroup of \(\prod_{\alpha\in I}A_\alpha\) defined as
\begin{equation*} \bigoplus\limits_{\alpha\in I}A_\alpha=\{(g_\alpha)\in \prod_{\alpha\in I}A_\alpha\colon g_{\alpha}=0_\alpha \text{ for all but finitely many } \alpha\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
