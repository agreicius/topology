A Euclidean ball \(B\subseteq M\) is regular is there is a Euclidean ball \(B'\) such that (i) \(\overline{B}\subseteq B'\) and (ii) there is a homeomorphism of \(\phi\colon B'\rightarrow B_{2r}(\boldzero)\subseteq \R^n\) for some \(r> 0\text{,}\) such that \(\overline{B}=\phi^{-1}(\overline{B}_r(\boldzero))\text{.}\)
