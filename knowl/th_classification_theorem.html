Theorem 1.20.5. Classification theorem.

Let \(T_n\) denote the \(n\)-fold torus, and let \(P_m\) denote the \(m\)-fold projective plane. If \(X\) is a compact 2-manifold (i.e., a surface), then \(X\) is homemorphic to exactly one of the following surfaces: \(S^2\text{,}\) \(T_n\) for some \(n\geq 1\text{,}\) \(P_m\) for some \(m\geq 1\text{.}\)
