Definition 2.12.3. Direct product of groups.

Given a family \(\{G_\alpha\}_{\alpha\in I}\) of groups, its direct product \(\prod_{\alpha\in I}G_\alpha\) is the group with underlying set
\begin{equation*} \prod_{\alpha\in I}G_{\alpha}=\{(g_\alpha)_{\alpha\in I}\colon g_\alpha\in G_\alpha\} \end{equation*}
and group operation
\begin{equation*} (g_\alpha)\cdot (h_\alpha)=(g_\alpha\cdot h_{\alpha})\text{.} \end{equation*}
