The bijection (1.21.1) “respects arrows” in the following sense. Suppose the equivalence classes \([E\xrightarrow{p} B]\) and \([E'\xrightarrow{p'}B]\) correspond to the conjugacy classes \([H]\) and \([H']\) via (1.21.1). There is a covering \(h\colon E\rightarrow E'\) satisfying \(p=p'\circ h\) if and only if \(H\leq gH'g^{-1}\) for some \(g\in G\text{.}\)
